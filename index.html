  • 评估1983年后改革（塞地贬值、CMB重组、技术应用）的经济影响，量化可可产量（从24万吨增至80多万吨）和贫困率下降（51.7%至39.5%）的改善。

Work-From-Home Effects Evaluation

远程工作影响评估

  • Conducted a two-step econometric analysis of a Chinese travel agency's WFH pilot, leveraging productivity metrics (call volume/quality), retention rates, and satisfaction surveys.
  • Applied difference-in-differences (DID) models to measure causal impacts on productivity and satisfaction, with standard regressions for retention, controlling for baseline characteristics.
  • Validated randomization through covariate balance tests, cleaned 6-month employee datasets, and implemented robust error clustering to address heteroskedasticity.
  • Found no significant change in productivity, a 13.6% reduction in quit rates (p<0.01), and notable satisfaction improvements (job +0.6, life +5.1, career +3.2 on 10-point scales), supporting WFH as a retention and well-being enhancer.
  • 对中国某旅行社的远程工作试点进行两步计量分析，利用生产力指标（通话量/质量）、留存率和满意度调查数据。
  • 应用双重差分（DID）模型衡量对生产力和满意度的因果影响，用标准回归分析留存率，控制基线特征。
  • 通过协变量平衡测试验证随机化，清理6个月员工数据集，并实施稳健误差聚类处理异方差性。
  • 发现生产力无显著变化，离职率下降13.6%(p<0.01)，满意度显著提升（工作+0.6、生活+5.1、职业发展+3.2，10分制），证明远程工作可提高留存率和福祉。
